## The fellowship

The [Ethereum Protocol Fellowship](https://github.com/eth-protocol-fellows/cohort-seven)
(EPF) brings developers into Ethereum core-protocol work. Fellows spend the cohort
on a focused project alongside client teams and researchers, and they post
biweekly dev updates on their progress. This site collects mine.
